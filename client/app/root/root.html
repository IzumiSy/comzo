Comzo: All-in-one crowd analyzing platform

This is the small demo webapp that shows how Comzo can be used. Comzo is the advanced cutting-edge all-in-one analyzing platform that takes advantage of various type of data on the internet. Cozmo automatically gathers the piled up miscellaneous data which possibly make affection to the behavior of the people in the world or in the specific place, and also generates easy-modifiable/manipulatable summary data as Web API.
